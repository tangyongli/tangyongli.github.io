Smallholder Paddy Rice Mapping in Mountainous areas

For smallholder paddy rice mapping in mountainous areas, we designed a dual-branch CNN: one branch is CNN2D for spatial information extraction, which detects edges, textures, and shapes of paddy fields. The other branch is CNN1D for spectral information extraction, which focuses more on small objects as it only considers the pixel itself.
At the end of each branch, spatial and spectral attention are conducted, respectively.
To pay more attention to important features, we used an attention block to fuse the features from the two branches.
